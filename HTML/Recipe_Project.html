My Favorite Recipes

Tamrind rice recipe. Follow the steps:

  1. Add oil in the pan
  2. Fry red chillies and green chilies
  3. Fry pop in the oil and ground nuts
  4. Add some salt and turmeric powder
  5. Add the tamirind juice and heat it until the water ouzes out
  6. Mix boiled rice in it

Tomato rice recipe. Follow the steps:

  1. Add oil in the pan
  2. Fry red chillies and green chilies
  3. Fry pop in the oil
  4. Add some salt and turmeric powder
  5. Fry onions and tomatoes and then add chilli powder, garam masala and coriander powder
  6. Mix boiled rice in it
